HERO

ABOUT

Shannon Basso Gaule is a super talented man of comedy and beards.

Beginning his comedy career in New Zealand, Shannon gigged up and down the country making appearances in the Wellington & Whangerai Fringe and Palmy Comedy Fest.

In 2020 he was awarded Best Newcomer of the Lower North Island by the NZ Comedy Guild, nominated for Best Male comedian - Cartel Comedy and Best Out Of Towner by The Comedy Hub.

Since his return to Ireland in late 2021, he’s performed as a finalist in Show Me the Funny and Cherry Comedy Breakout Act. He performed in Barcelona alongside Michelle Wolfe. He’s also produced his own shows in his local Carlow.

He’s currently storming gigs all over Ireland and Europe.
